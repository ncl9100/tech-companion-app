Privacy Policy for ElderlyAI

Last updated: July 19, 2025

Summary: ElderlyAI is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal data to improve your experience and do not collect any personal information like names, emails, or location data.

Information We Collect

Local Device Data (Stored on Your Device Only)

  • App Preferences: Font size settings (18-28px) and speech rate settings (0.7-1.3x)
  • Learning Progress: Which tutorial guides you have completed or started reading
  • Favorites: AI responses you choose to save for future reference

This data is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

Third-Party Services

  • OpenAI API: When you ask questions to our AI assistant, your question is sent to OpenAI's servers to generate helpful responses. We do not store your questions on our servers.
  • News API: We fetch AI-related news articles to keep you informed about technology developments.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide personalized learning experiences
  • To remember your preferences and progress
  • To generate helpful AI responses to your questions
  • To show relevant technology news and updates

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your information to third parties except:

  • When required by law
  • To protect our rights and safety
  • To OpenAI and News API services (only the specific data needed for their services)

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information. Your local data is stored securely on your device, and we use secure connections when communicating with third-party services.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your local data through the app settings
  • Clear your learning progress and favorites at any time
  • Delete the app, which will remove all local data
  • Contact us with privacy concerns

Children's Privacy

Our app is designed for elderly users and does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us immediately.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
